College Lister

Why?

We noticed a lack of centralised platform for students to search and discover colleges. When searching for colleges, students are bombarded with advertisments, making it difficult for them to make well informed decision about their education.

Our Solution

We, at College Lister believe knowledge is power.We want to help the students find the best college for them, without any distractions. We have no advertisments and no motive to push you into choosing a college. We simply show you a list of colleges, and their ranking.
